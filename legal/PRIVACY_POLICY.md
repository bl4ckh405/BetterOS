# Privacy Policy for BetterOS

**Last Updated:** January 2026

## 1. Introduction

BetterOS ("we," "our," or "us") provides a context-aware life operating system application. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application and services.

**By using BetterOS, you acknowledge that you have read and understood this Privacy Policy and consent to the collection and processing of your data as described herein.**

## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 Information You Provide Directly

- **Onboarding Data:** Core values, goals (5-year, 1-year, 10-year), anxieties, work style, motivation type
- **User-Generated Content:** Tasks, habits, entries, voice recordings, notes, and all conversational data
- **Account Information:** username, profile preferences
- **Payment Information:** Processed through third-party payment processors (RevenueCat)

### 2.2 Automatically Collected Information

- **Usage Data:** App interactions, feature usage, session duration, mode preferences
- **Device Information:** Device type, operating system, unique device identifiers, mobile network information
- **Audio Data:** Voice recordings when using voice-first features (processed and may be stored)

### 2.3 Third-Party Data

- **AI Service Providers:** Data shared with Google (Gemini AI), ElevenLabs (voice processing), and other AI providers
- **Analytics:** Data collected through analytics services to improve app performance

## 3. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information for:

- **Service Delivery:** Providing personalized AI coaching, context-aware responses, and app functionality
- **AI Training:** Your data may be used to improve AI models and service quality
- **Product Improvement:** Analyzing usage patterns to enhance features
- **Communication:** Sending notifications, updates, and promotional materials
- **Legal Compliance:** Complying with legal obligations and enforcing our terms
- **Research:** Aggregated and anonymized data for research purposes
